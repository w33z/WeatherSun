# WeatherSun !

It's simply app for displaying current weather, air quality in your city and also forecast for next 9 days. You can change temperature unit  or city for any place on the world!


# Cocoapods

Alamofire  
BetterSegmentedControl  
GooglePlaces  
GooglePlacePicker   
GoogleMaps
